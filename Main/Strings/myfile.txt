The butterfly effect is a scientific conceptual theory that a single occurrence.
no matter how small it may seem to be, can result in a course change in the entire universe.
It further suggests that if one could be able to go back in the past.
 and the something even if was negligible by then.
their entire life would be affected and thereby a change. 
justification of this.
is the grand power of a butterfly flapping it wings in New Mexico.
and the effect however small it was caused a hurricane in China. 
The concept was used in weather forecasting by a renowned meteorologist.
Ed Lorenz who incorporated computers to forecast the atmospheric conditions using the butterfly effect theory.
With time-drive, the theory gained momentum to central focus and application in today's science.
